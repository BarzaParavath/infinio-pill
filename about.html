Our Story

It all started with a small group of friends who shared one big question: Why do we spend one third of our lives just sleeping? We’d see people with ideas, goals, and dreams but by the time they found the energy to chase them, the day was already over. It didn’t feel right. There had to be a better way. So, we decided to create something that could change that. We spent countless nights researching, experimenting, and failing more times than we can count. But every failure only made us more certain that we were onto something important. Then came the breakthrough. The moment we realized we had built something that could help people live life fully awake without trading their health or focus to do it. That’s how INFINIO PILL was born. Not from luck, but from passion. From the belief that time shouldn’t hold anyone back from what they want to do or who they want to become.
